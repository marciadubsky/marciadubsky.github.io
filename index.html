Marcia Dubsky

Writer, Journalist & Freelancer

My name is Marcia Dubsky and I am a Brazilian storyteller in Canada. I won an award back in 2019 for an essay video talking about my faith and got invited to be on air for 30 minutes at Radio Maria Canada.

I had the privilege to work with Macleans Magazine on #TheyWereLoved project back in 2020. This gave me the opportunity to pay tribute to everyone who has died of COVID-19 in Canada. I currently write for the Observer News and have my own Youtube channel, where I share my story and ideas and interview people in Portuguese and English. Don’t forget to check it out!
